Music Favorites Guide

Add Entry: Tap the + button to add albums, playlists, or singles. Rate them 1-5 stars.

Filter: Use the filter buttons to view by rating, type, or special categories (Holiday, Hypnosis, Sleep).

Search: Search by artist or title. Tap ✕ to clear.

Edit: Tap any entry to edit or delete. Drag to reorder (edit mode).

Sections: Tap headers to collapse/expand. Use Expand All / Collapse All buttons.

Export/Import: Backup your data anytime. Import from a previously exported file.

Tap any entry to open in Apple Music.
